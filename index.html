Progressive Tax System

Your total income is divided into "buckets" and taxed at a different rate. Here are the first two buckets for federal and California taxes (2025 rates).

Federal Tax Brackets

  • **Standard Deduction:** The first ~$14,600 of your income is not taxed.
  • **10% Tax Rate:** The next ~$11,600 of your income is taxed at 10%.

California Tax Brackets

  • **1% Tax Rate:** The first ~$10,412 of your income is taxed at 1%.
  • **2% Tax Rate:** The next ~$14,272 of your income is taxed at 2%.

Hypothetical Paycheck

Here's how it works for a **monthly paycheck** based on working 80 hours a month (20 hours per week) at $16.50/hour.

Gross Monthly Pay: $1,320.00
Federal Income Tax: $10.33
California State Tax: $8.43
Social Security (6.2%): $81.84
Medicare (1.45%): $19.14
California SDI (1.2%): $15.84
Total Deductions: $135.58
Net Pay (Take-Home): $1,184.42

*As a dependent, your standard deduction is limited. The actual tax you pay on your own return may be affected. The numbers above are for illustrative purposes.
